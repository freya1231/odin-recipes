Carbonara recipe

Description

Gennaro's classic spaghetti carbonara - CREAMY, SMOKY & INDULGENT

Ingredients

  • 3 large free-range egg yolks
  • g Parmesan cheese , plus extra to serve
  • 1 x 150 g piece of higher-welfare pancetta
  • 200 g dried spaghetti
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • extra virgin olive oil

Method

  1. Put the egg yolks into a bowl, finely grate in the Parmesan, season with pepper, then mix well with a fork and put to one side.
  2. Serve with a grating of Parmesan and an extra twist of pepper.
